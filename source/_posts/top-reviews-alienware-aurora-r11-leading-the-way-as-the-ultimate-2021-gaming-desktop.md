## HAVIT 5 vs. Kootak Laptop Cooling Pad

 It makes sense to compare the Kootak Laptop Cooling Pad to the HAVIT. If you prefer a true gaming cooling pad, go with the HAVIT. If you don‚Äôt care about aesthetics and prefer to be cost conscious, the Kootak will be better suited to your needs.
