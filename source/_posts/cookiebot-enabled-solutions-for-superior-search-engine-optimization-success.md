 The device will remain locked by the Activation Lock. It means even if you erase your iPhone through Recovery mode before turning it on, it won‚Äôt surpass the country selection screen. There will be a prompt to login with the Apple ID and password.

#### **3\. What happens when you erase your phone from Find My iPhone?**
